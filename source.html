Why choose these data?

These data are both published by Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, and they have the format of CSV which is easy to edit with, and they have formal license as well as they are open to the public. These data are offering the covid cases by sorting with states, sex, age since the time when covid took places in U.S..

Also, there are additional json files are found to create the map, which is helpful when generating the geocharts for representing.
